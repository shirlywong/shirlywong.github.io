About

Hello! My name is Shirley! I have several nicknames that have stuck with my closest friends, “shin,” “shirl,” “squirreley,” “swirley,” “sheen,” “shilly.” Honestly call me whichever nickname you like. I am a hobbyist artist in London, Ontario. I love comics, animation, games, film, history, music, food, tea… as you can see I have a wide range of interests, I’m sure I can find a common interest with just about anyone. My work is inspired by all these things I love, my friends, my family, what I study, and existing in this current time and space. Hope we can get along!
